# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built to monitor revenue, profit, and sales performance across regions, countries, item types, and sales channels. This dashboard provides interactive visualizations to help identify trends, top-performing markets, and business insights.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The **Sales Analysis Dashboard** helps businesses analyze sales performance using interactive charts and KPIs. Users can filter data by region, country, month, and item type to gain deeper insights into sales trends and profitability.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

The dashboard highlights important business KPIs:

- **Total Profit:** 196.24 Billion  
- **Total Revenue:** 665.05 Billion

## 📈 Features

### 1️⃣ KPI Indicators
Displays overall business performance including:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Units Sold

### 2️⃣ Revenue Trend Analysis
- Monthly revenue trend visualization
- Helps track sales seasonality and fluctuations.

### 3️⃣ Regional Sales Insights
Analyze sales performance by:
- Region
- Country
- Item Type

### 4️⃣ Sales Channel Comparison
Compare revenue between:
- **Online Sales**
- **Offline Sales**

### 5️⃣ Order Priority Distribution
Visual breakdown of orders based on priority levels:
- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

### 6️⃣ Top Countries by Units Sold
Identify the **top-performing countries** in terms of sales volume.

### 7️⃣ Revenue by Item Type
Shows revenue contributions from product categories such as:
- Baby Food
- Beverages
- Cereal
- Clothes
- Cosmetics
- Fruits
- Household
- Meat
- Office Supplies
- Personal Care

---

## 🎛 Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard by:

- **Region**
- **Country**

- **Item Type**
- **Sales Channel**

These filters allow users to explore the data and generate customized insights.
